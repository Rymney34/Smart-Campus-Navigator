About Campus Navigator

Introduction

Campus Navigator is a web-based navigation system built to help students, staff, and visitors explore and find their way around the Cardiff Metropolitan University Llandaff campus. Whether you're new to campus or simply trying to locate a specific building or room, our platform offers an intuitive and accessible experience to make campus life easier.

Website Features

  • Interactive Campus Map: Explore a detailed map of the campus with clickable buildings.
  • Building Details: View room-level information inside buildings, including classrooms, labs, offices, and available facilities.
  • Real-Time Navigation: Get real-time, live directions from your current location to any building or facility on campus.
  • Search Functionality: Quickly find specific buildings, rooms, or facilities using our search feature.
  • Mobile-Friendly Interface: Fully responsive layout so you can access navigation and campus info on the go.
